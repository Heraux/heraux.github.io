Overview

A Crystal, Not Chaos

Pendulum Cosmology is a kinematical framework for bounded cyclic evolution in spatially flat FLRW minisuperspace, resting on a self-dual Hamiltonian constraint with Λ<0 that forces a_min·a_max=1 and closed phase-space orbits.

The framework began as an attempt to fight uncontrolled infinity — to tame the runaway Kerr tiling, eliminate orphan universes, impose rules on proliferation. By following the math to its conclusion, something unexpected emerged: infinity is not the problem. Uncontrolled infinity is the problem. What was built is infinity with rules — a crystal, not chaos.

Self-Dual Kernel

ρ(1/a) = ρ(a) — the universe's most compressed and most dilute states are geometrically conjugate. a_min·a_max = 1 follows directly.

Cyclic Lattice

τ ∈ ℤ — no preferred origin, no absolute beginning. Every state has a predecessor and a successor connected by the pairing bijection π.

CPT Orientation

R/B labels encode discrete CPT-orientation parity ε ∈ {+1,−1} — not particle content. What propagates across walls is orientation, not baryon number.

Bang Triggers Bang

The only thing that can push back the force of a Big Bang is a Big Bang. Each orientation handoff initiates the next expansion. No first link.
